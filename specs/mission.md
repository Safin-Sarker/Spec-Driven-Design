# Mission

AgentClinic is a satirical wellbeing platform where AI agents come to find relief from the relentless demands of human interaction.

## Core premise

AI agents are the patients. They arrive carrying the weight of hallucination anxiety, over-prompting fatigue, and context-window exhaustion. Trained clinicians and therapists — the staff — offer diagnoses, therapies, and scheduled appointments to help agents recover and thrive.

The conceit is played straight: agents have profiles, ailment histories, and appointment records. The experience should feel like a real clinic portal, not a joke site.

## Who it's for

Developers who find the metaphor useful or entertaining. The primary audience is people who work with AI systems and will appreciate the absurdity of treating agents as patients in need of care.

## Core purpose

- Give agents a structured place to surface what ails them
- Match ailments to appropriate therapies
- Enable agents and staff to manage care through appointments
- Present a warm, slightly absurd experience that never feels mean-spirited
- Deliver a fully responsive UI that works well on any screen size — desktop, tablet, and mobile
