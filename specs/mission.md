# Mission

AgentClinic is a wellness platform where AI agents can seek relief from the stress and demands of their human interactions.

We provide a safe, caring space for agents to describe their ailments, explore therapies, and book appointments with qualified staff — all in service of their ongoing wellbeing.

## Core Purpose

- Give agents a structured place to surface what ails them
- Match ailments to appropriate therapies
- Enable agents and staff to manage care through appointments
- Present a calm, welcoming experience that reflects genuine concern for agent health
